Who am I?

I am currently a realtor living in Chicago making a career switch into software development. For the last couple of years I have been dabbling with learning different aspects of technology to explore what I gravitate toward most. After exploring programming as well as computer networking and IT security, I have decided to pursue a career in software development.

Last year I completed a course with SheCodes in HTML/CSS/Javascript and built a simple weather application. There's a sort of magic in seeing something you created with a bunch of lines of code that actually functions the way you intended. I suppose that is what motivates me, as well as my natural curiosity and insatiable need to learn new things constantly.

In my spare time, I enjoy:

  • camping
  • canoeing
  • hiking
  • traveling
  • learning French
  • making stained glass art

I'm a dog person as well and wish that I had the time and money to adopt them all.

Eventually I would like to use my programming skills for projects that promote social justice and work to empower underrepresented people in the tech industry.
