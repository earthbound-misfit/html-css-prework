About Me

I am a realtor living in Chicago making a career switch into software development.

For the last couple of years I have been dabbling with learning different aspects of technology to explore what I gravitate toward most. After exploring programming and computer networking and IT security, I have decided to pursue a career in software engineering.

I have always had a seemingly insatiable need to continue learning. In the past couple of years, I have been learning French, as well as how to make stained glass art and how to sew.

Eventually I would like to use my programming skills for projects that promote social justice and work to empower underrepresented people in the tech industry.
